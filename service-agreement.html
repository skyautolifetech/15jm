Scope of Services

AutoLifeTech will provide professional planning, consultation, and device recommendations across the following domains. All recommendations will be coordinated with the Client's appointed electrical company for implementation.

📱 A. Smart Device Planning

Device selection, connectivity, and placement recommendations for the entire property.

  • Device type selection & specification
  • Connectivity planning (Zigbee, Z-Wave, WiFi, Thread, Matter)
  • Location planning & placement optimization
  • Smart lighting (switches, dimmers, LED strips, motion sensors)
  • Smart plugs & power monitoring
  • Smart locks & access control (keypad, fingerprint, NFC)
  • Smart blinds & motorized curtains
  • Smart aircon & fan control
  • Smart speakers & voice assistants
  • Smart sensors (door/window, motion, water leak, smoke, humidity)
  • Water irrigation control (smart sprinklers, soil moisture sensors, zone scheduling)
  • Smart garage door control

⚡ B. Electrical Planning

Electrical requirements and recommendations to be coordinated with the appointed electrician.

  • Electrical load assessment for smart devices
  • Dedicated circuit recommendations
  • Smart panel / smart breaker recommendations
  • EV charger placement planning
  • Backup power (UPS) for critical systems
  • Outlet placement for smart devices
  • Low-voltage wiring recommendations (12V/24V DC)
  • Surge protection planning

🤖 C. AI & Automation

Automation design and AI-powered features for an intelligent, adaptive home.

  • Automation rule design (scenes, routines, schedules)
  • Voice assistant ecosystem planning
  • AI camera analytics (person detection, LPR, package detection)
  • Geofencing automation (arrive/leave triggers)
  • Smart notification & alert setup
  • Presence detection & occupancy awareness

💼 D. Home Business Office

Professional home office optimized for productivity and business operations.

  • Dedicated network segment for business
  • Video conferencing optimization (lighting, audio)
  • UPS / power backup for office equipment
  • Smart lighting for video calls
  • Printer & scanner network integration
  • Secure remote access planning

🦾 E. Robotics

Autonomous robotic systems for household maintenance and upkeep.

  • Robot vacuum & mop planning (mapping, scheduling)
  • Lawn mowing robot planning
  • Pool cleaning robot integration
  • Charging station placement & power planning
  • Integration with home automation platform

🎬 F. Electronics & Entertainment

Audio, video, and entertainment system recommendations for the home.

  • Home theater / media room planning
  • Multi-room audio system design
  • TV & display integration
  • Outdoor entertainment (weatherproof speakers, outdoor TV)
  • Intercom & PA system planning
  • HDMI distribution & AV switching

🛡 G. Safety & Security

Security and life-safety system planning for comprehensive property protection.

  • CCTV camera placement planning (indoor & outdoor)
  • AI-powered surveillance (person, vehicle detection)
  • License plate recognition (LPR) system
  • Smart doorbell / video intercom planning
  • Alarm system recommendations (intrusion, panic)
  • Smart smoke & CO detectors
  • Water leak detection sensors
  • Security camera storage planning (NVR vs cloud)
  • Surge & lightning protection for smart systems

🌐 H. Network & WiFi

Reliable network infrastructure design for all smart devices and connectivity needs.

  • Network topology design (wired + wireless)
  • WiFi access point placement & coverage planning
  • Network switch & rack planning
  • VLAN segmentation (IoT, business, guest, CCTV)
  • Firewall & security planning
  • PoE (Power over Ethernet) planning
  • Smart home hub selection
  • Structured cabling plan (CAT6a / fiber)
  • Outdoor WiFi coverage (garden, driveway)

Terms & Conditions

The following terms govern this engagement between AutoLifeTech and the Client.

  • Agreement Value

    The total fee for the Smart Home Architect service is SGD $88,000, payable across three milestones as detailed in Section 2.

  • Payment Terms

    Each milestone payment is due upon delivery of the corresponding deliverables. Payment is to be made via bank transfer or PayNow within 7 days of invoice date.
