Custom-Coded Parcel Delivery Controller

A custom-programmed ESP32-based relay controller (Kincony B8 hardware) with bespoke firmware developed specifically for this parcel delivery system.

  • 8 relay channels — each compartment independently controlled
  • 12/24VDC output to electric lock solenoids
  • Custom firmware with PIN-to-relay mapping logic
  • WiFi, Ethernet & Bluetooth connectivity
  • MQTT integration with Home Assistant for notifications
  • Over-the-air firmware updates
🔐

Keypad PIN Access

Dedicated PIN codes allow delivery personnel to open specific compartments without entering the property or interacting with anyone.

  • Unique PIN per delivery box
  • Temporary / one-time PINs supported
  • Audit trail of all access events with timestamps
  • Remote PIN management via app
🧑

Facial Recognition (Advanced)

For recurring delivery personnel (e.g. your regular courier), AI facial recognition can unlock the designated compartment hands-free.

  • Enroll trusted, recurring delivery drivers
  • Camera identifies face → auto-unlocks assigned box
  • Integrated with UniFi AI camera system
  • Fallback to keypad PIN if not recognized

Special Features via Custom-Coded Firmware

Beyond standard PIN access, the custom firmware enables advanced unlock methods tailored to different delivery scenarios.

⏰

Time-Based Unlock

Schedule a delivery compartment to automatically unlock during a specific time window — ideal for deliveries with a known arrival time.

  • Set an unlock window (e.g. 2:00 PM – 3:00 PM)
  • Box is accessible only during that period
  • Auto-locks when the time window expires
  • Perfect for scheduled grocery or meal deliveries
🚘

License Plate Unlock

If the delivery vehicle is always the same, the system can recognize its license plate and automatically unlock the assigned box on arrival.

  • Register a delivery vehicle's plate number
  • AI camera detects plate → triggers relay unlock
  • No PIN or interaction needed by the driver
  • Works with UniFi AI CCTV LPR from UX 2
🔑

One-Time PIN (OTP)

Generate a single-use PIN code for one-off deliveries. Once used, the code is immediately invalidated — it cannot be reused.

  • Generate OTP via the app and share with courier
  • PIN works once, then is permanently expired
  • Ideal for ad-hoc or first-time deliveries
  • Full audit log of OTP generation & usage

Requirements

Additional Infrastructure Needed

The following wiring and infrastructure must be installed to support all three systems.

2× CAT6a Ethernet Cables

Shielded Category 6a cables for data communication between UniFi controller, AI cameras, intercom, and the parcel delivery controller. Supports PoE for powering devices.

2.5mm² Power Cable

Dedicated power wiring to supply 12/24VDC from the PSU to the relay controller and each electric lock solenoid in the delivery compartments and gates.

Conduit & Protection

Outdoor-rated conduit to protect cable runs from weather exposure along the front wall panel, from the network cabinet to each compartment lock point.

12V / 24V DC Power Supply

A DIN-rail mountable DC power supply unit installed in the network compartment to power the delivery controller and all connected electric lock mechanisms.
